Privacy Our privacy statement

  1. Home
  2. Privacy

We collect personal information from you, including information about your:

  • Name
  • Contact information
  • Location
  • Interactions with us
  • Billing or purchase information

We collect your personal information in order to:

  • Make reservations
  • Answer inquiries or to share experiences

Providing some information is optional. If you choose not to enter their name, mobile number or email address, we'll be unable to confirm their reservations.. We keep your information safe by storing it on our secure cloud-based servers and only allowing appropriate staff to review this information.

We keep your information for two years at which point we securely destroy it by securely erasing all digital data.

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong. If you’d like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at administrator@haukai.co.nz, or 09-401-4019, or 1 Island Street, Kerikeri, Bay of Islands.
